About Our Parish

A Living Testament to
Centuries of Faith

Thumpoly St. Thomas Church is a historic parish in Alappuzha, Kerala. Founded in 1580, it stands as a testament to over four centuries of unbroken faith, apostolic tradition, and devoted community service.

Rooted in the legacy of St. Thomas the Apostle who brought Christianity to Kerala in 52 AD, our parish remains a spiritual home for thousands of faithful families across generations.
